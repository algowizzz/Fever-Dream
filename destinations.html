INDIA

India is a land of contrasts, where ancient history and modernity coexist in a vibrant kaleidoscope of cultures, religions, and landscapes. Whether you want to marvel at the majestic Taj Mahal, the symbol of love and one of the wonders of the world, or experience the spiritual bliss of the holy Ganges river, India has something for everyone. You can also explore the rich wildlife of India, home to the largest population of tigers in the world, or enjoy the scenic beauty of the Himalayas, the world's highest mountain range.
